About Buildsurge Irrigation

A growing HDPE pipe manufacturer in Pune, Maharashtra committed to quality infrastructure solutions for agriculture and industry.

Our Story

From Pune to Maharashtra's Fields

Incorporated on 8 September 2022, Buildsurge Irrigation Private Limited was founded with a clear vision: to manufacture high-quality HDPE pipes that power India's agricultural infrastructure.

Registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (ROC: Pune), the company operates as an active private limited entity with its Head Office in Balaji Nagar, Pune and a manufacturing plant in Jejuri, Maharashtra. This strategic dual presence allows us to combine corporate efficiency with dedicated production capacity.

In FY 2024, we crossed ₹4.12 Crore in revenue — a strong testament to growing market trust and our expanding customer base across Maharashtra's agricultural, infrastructure, and utility sectors.
