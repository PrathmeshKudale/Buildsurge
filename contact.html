FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from dealers, contractors, and procurement teams.

Do you supply retail or only bulk orders?
We focus exclusively on B2B bulk supply. Minimum order quantities apply. We do not operate a retail store or online shop.
What is the delivery area?
We supply across Maharashtra and can arrange transport delivery to other states for large orders. Delivery terms are discussed at the time of quotation.
What pressure ratings are available?
We offer PN 4, PN 6, PN 8, PN 10, PN 12.5, and PN 16 pressure ratings depending on the pipe size. Please specify your requirement in the inquiry.
Are pipes IS standard certified?
Yes, all our HDPE pipes are manufactured as per IS 4984 / IS 14333 standards. Material test certificates can be provided on request.
How long does it take to process a bulk order?
Lead times depend on order quantity and pipe sizes. Typical dispatch is within 7–21 working days from order confirmation. Large orders are discussed case by case.
