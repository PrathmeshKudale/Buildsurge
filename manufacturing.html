Production Process

How We Manufacture

A disciplined, step-by-step manufacturing process ensures every pipe that leaves our Jejuri plant meets the highest quality standards.

01

Raw Material Procurement

We source certified PE80/PE100 HDPE resin from approved suppliers with material test certificates. Every batch is verified before production begins.

02

Extrusion

HDPE resin is melted and extruded through precision dies to form continuous pipe profiles. Screw speeds, temperatures, and die settings are closely monitored.

03

Sizing & Cooling

Extruded pipes pass through sizing calibrators and water cooling tanks to achieve precise outer diameter and wall thickness as per IS standards.

04

In-Line Inspection

Diameter, wall thickness, and surface finish are continuously monitored during production using inline measurement instruments.

05

Cutting & Coiling

Pipes are cut to standard lengths (6m / 12m) or coiled as per customer requirement, then end-capped for protection.

06

Final QC & Dispatch

Finished pipes undergo hydrostatic pressure testing, visual inspection, and marking before being stacked and dispatched from our plant.

Quality Assurance
